Why Attend Navgati?

Navgati is a celebration of culture, creativity, and collaboration that reflects the vibrant life of maritime cadets beyond academics. The fest brings together students from diverse backgrounds to participate in engaging quizzes, artistic showcases, interactive games, and stage performances. Each event is designed to encourage confidence, teamwork, and self-expression while creating unforgettable memories. Navgati provides a unique platform to connect with peers, explore hidden talents, and experience healthy competition in a spirited atmosphere. It is where discipline meets creativity, friendships are forged, and moments turn into milestones.
